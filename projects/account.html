Account Opening Redesign

Rethinking the account opening flow and improving the experience

background

What is iQianjin

iqianjin help users to aquire financial investment product knowledge, create an investment account and generate profits.



INITIAL PROBLEMS

  1. The conversion rate of clicking open button to opening an account was below expectatioos
  2. Opening of account issues were reported from customer feedback department

lead designer

As the project lead designer, I built the initial team and collaborated with PM, multiple engineers.



user research

goals

CHALLENGES

approaches

UNDERSTAND EXISTING PROBLEMS

  • The location, explanation and necessity of bank logo
  • Not provide bank options
  • Open account tasks were not clearly dispalyed and lack of guidance


BUILD USER RESEARCH TEAM


Account opening is an essential step for investment. We had sufficient time to conduct qualitative research to establish the issues affecting the existing account opening process and we chose to perform usablity testing.

I set up the user goal is that: Is it clear about the flow from open account to purchase product? Intruduce team members, goal and process. Provide users tasks, observe the actions users make and record any questions or suggestions user give.

Build team

Define user tasks

Observe users

We invited 5 users from our target user group who have less, none and more Peer to peer financial investment background to explore different insights based on different financial investment education knowledge



We discovered more problems related to the process of purchasing products, the product detail page, ID identification. However, we only focused on the account opening page and as our direct goal was to solve the account opening problem


Insights

  • Most users only consider Huaxia card to open the account(4/5)
  • User only consider Huaxia card as the large bank logo at top from frist sight
  • Users don't know there are choices for other bank cards
  • User hardly locate the frist step and next step
  • Users confused about the page titled “open bank manage account’ and do not know what does that mean

design process

How might we reduce the confusion about the Huaxia bank card?

What if we change OR delete the previous bank logo location?

we changed the logo location and simplized the content to see if users were still confused about the card issue (as it's the easiest way to test it)

In order to control variables, we only changed one element, so we did internal testing and the feedback was positive and users no longer thought that they could only choose the Huaxia card. We shipped quickly because this page was easy to develop. There were no negative results for account opening rate and customer feedback, so we decided to move on to the next step and consider other solutions
