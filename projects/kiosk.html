Kiosk

In 2019, Zhangyue launched iReader Kiosks targeting enterprise level clients with the mission of making employee enjoy reading. We aimed to provide a delightful end to end reading experience to users by leveraging Kiosks to help discover new content and guide users to download the mobile app and complete the reading. I led the effort of redesigning the Kiosk's user interface to be more tailored to meet users' real life scenario after conducting a user research. We managed to increase keyboard meaningful usage rate by 10% and Zhangyue enterprise app download by 2%.
